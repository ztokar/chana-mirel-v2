Common questions

What should I wear?

Solid colors photograph best. Bring two to three options and we'll pick on the day. Avoid tight stripes and large logos.

Do you retouch the photos?

Yes. Skin, stray hair, wardrobe wrinkles, blemishes. I do not over-process — you should still look like you.

Can I bring multiple outfits?

Standard headshot session is one look. Personal-branding tier includes two to three. Changing rooms in the studio.

How fast do I get the photos?

Final retouched files within seven days. Rush turnaround available.
