A few clarifications from your feedback

  • Instagram: Will be linked in the header and footer of every page. No separate page needed.
  • Navigation: Each service will have its own clickable link at the top of the page, not hidden inside a subcategory.

  • In-home sessions: Will be mentioned as an add-on for an extra fee on the relevant service pages, not positioned as a main offering.
