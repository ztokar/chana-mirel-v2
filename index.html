About Chana

Photography that's meaningful, classy, and honest.

Chana Mirel Photography is a fine-art studio on Sweet Meadow Road. I specialize in babies in their first year, maternity in the final trimester, and family portraits that aren't stiff. Clients drive in from Canton, Towson, Owings Mills, and across Baltimore County. The studio is private, warm, and built for small humans who need extra patience.

Newborn sessions

The first two weeks, unhurried.

Newborn sessions happen between five and fourteen days old. Baby sleeps, we pose gently, parents rest on the couch. Sessions run two to three hours. Wraps, bonnets, and props are provided.

Maternity sessions

Studio maternity, third trimester.

Maternity sessions are studio-only, scheduled between weeks 32 and 36. Gowns and drapes are on site. Sessions are private, warm, and take about an hour. Partners and older children are welcome.

Cake smash & first birthday

Cake, milk bath, and a first-birthday portrait.

The cake smash bundle includes a clean first-birthday portrait, the cake moment, and a milk bath finish. One session, three looks. Outfits provided. Sessions take about ninety minutes and run on your baby's schedule.
