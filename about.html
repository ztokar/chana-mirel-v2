About

Hi, I'm Chana.

I'm a Baltimore studio photographer. I've spent the last eight years photographing newborns, pregnancy, and families who want something more meaningful than a quick session at the mall.

The studio

Built for tiny humans.

My studio sits on Sweet Meadow Road in Pikesville, 21209. It's private, warm, and stocked with everything a newborn session needs. Wraps, bonnets, buckets, backdrops, and a lot of patience. No rushing, no freezing metal chairs, no shared waiting rooms.

Families drive in from Canton, Towson, Owings Mills, and across Baltimore County. The studio parking is right out front.

The approach

Meaningful. Classy. Honest.

I photograph real families. That means no forced smiles, no stiff poses, no filters that make a baby's skin look like plastic. The final gallery is a blend of the portraits you wanted and the moments that happened anyway.

My work is fine-art. Clean backdrops, honest light, careful retouching. Every file is delivered print-ready.

The experience

Eight years. Five-hundred families.

I've photographed over five hundred Baltimore families across eight years. I've got a hundred-plus five-star reviews on Google. I get sent wedding invitations, bar mitzvah photos, and annual cards from clients who came to me as nervous first-time parents.

Your session will be calm. Your gallery will be honest. Your wall art will be beautiful. That's the promise.
