Sports Portraits

Baltimore sports portraits.

Sports portraits, or Sportraits, are studio sessions for young athletes. No on-field action, no in-game coverage. Dancers, gymnasts, baseball, soccer, whatever the sport. Clean studio light, the uniform you already own, and a portrait that looks like a baseball card.

Inquire about a session

How the shoot runs

Forty-five minutes, studio light.

I bring the lights and the backdrop. You bring the uniform, any props (helmet, bat, ribbon, glove, ball), and a variation outfit if you have one. Home jersey plus away jersey, for example. We cycle through looks and I pose as we go.

Sessions run about forty-five minutes. Any sport works. Dance and gymnastics look especially good on clean backdrops because the uniform is already the focal point.

The style

Sports glam, no cheesy overlays.

I call the style sports glam. Clean backdrops, editorial lighting, zero of those overlay graphics that date instantly. These portraits end up on team walls, in college application packets, and framed on grandparents' shelves.

Team group sessions are also on offer. Send me the sport, the roster, and roughly when you want to book. I'll quote a group session based on how many kids are in it.

Common questions

Frequently asked

What ages do you photograph?
Five through eighteen. Younger is welcome with parent support.
Can you photograph my whole team?
Yes. Send me the sport and roster size for a team quote.
What should my athlete bring?
Full uniform, equipment, and any variations (home and away jersey, dance costumes, ribbons).
How is this different from action photography at a game?
Studio sessions are styled portraits, not in-game coverage. The goal is a clean headshot-quality image, not a candid play.
When is turnaround?
One week after the session.
