Headshots & Personal Branding

Baltimore headshot photographer.

Headshots are quick. Thirty to sixty minutes, clean studio light, and you walk out with three to six final frames ready for LinkedIn, your speaker bio, or a company website. I turn them around in under a week.

Inquire about a session

The session

Thirty to sixty minutes.

I start with a quick conversation about what you're updating. LinkedIn? A speaker bio? A new company website? Your industry matters too — a hedge fund headshot and a yoga teacher headshot shouldn't look the same. I style from there.

You'll walk out with three to six final retouched headshots, plus a couple of half-body options if you want them. Turnaround is about a week.

Branding sessions

Beyond the headshot.

Personal branding is the longer format. Not just a headshot against a backdrop, but lifestyle portraits of you at a desk, with a laptop, holding a book, or working with a client. Coaches, authors, entrepreneurs — anyone who needs more than a square avatar.

These run longer. We talk through location, props, and wardrobe before you show up. I like to have a shot list so we're not figuring it out on the fly.

What's included

Every session

  • 01. 30–60 minute studio session (headshot) or 90 minutes (full branding).
  • 02. Wardrobe consultation before the session.
  • 03. Professionally retouched gallery in 5–7 business days.
  • 04. High-resolution digital files formatted for LinkedIn, web, and print.
  • 05. Full usage rights for personal and business use.
