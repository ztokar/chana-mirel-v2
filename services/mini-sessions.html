Mini Sessions

Baltimore mini sessions.

Mini sessions are short, themed, and seasonal. Twenty minutes per family, one themed backdrop, enough frames for a holiday card or a quick update portrait. I run them a few times a year around Christmas, Mother's Day, and fall.

Inquire about a session

The format

Twenty minutes per family.

The format is tight. Twenty minutes, one backdrop, maybe one outfit change if you're fast. You walk out with a handful of gallery-ready images, which is usually enough for a holiday card or an update portrait. You won't get a full family session in that time, and that's by design.

Mini days happen a few times a year. Christmas is the big one. Mother's Day and fall come next. I announce dates by email and on Instagram.

The catch

Slots go fast.

I only run a handful of mini slots per season, back-to-back on the same day. When the announcement goes out, the first people to reply usually fill it up. If you know you want one, get on the email list early.

Minis are also a decent first-time booking. Lower commitment than a full family session. Same studio, same retouching quality, just shorter.

What's included

Every session

  • 01. 20-minute studio session with Chana.
  • 02. Themed backdrop and styling for the season.
  • 03. Retouched digital gallery in 1–2 weeks.
  • 04. Holiday-ready image sizes for cards and social.
  • 05. Print ordering from the gallery.

Common questions

Frequently asked

When are mini sessions available?
Christmas, Mother's Day, and fall. Specific dates are announced each season.
How long is a mini session?
Twenty minutes. Plan to arrive five minutes early.
How many images do I get?
A curated gallery of 8–12 retouched images.
Can I book a regular session instead?
Yes. If the theme isn't what you're looking for, book a standard family session.
How do I hear about upcoming mini dates?
Follow on Instagram or send me a note asking to be added to the mini-session email list.
