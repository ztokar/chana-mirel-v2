Family Sessions

Baltimore family photographer.

Family sessions happen in the studio, not on location. Parents, kids, siblings, sometimes grandparents too. I don't rush these. The first fifteen minutes are usually kids warming up. The good shots come after that.

Inquire about a session

How it runs

Sixty to ninety minutes.

Family sessions take about an hour, sometimes a bit more. The first fifteen minutes are kids getting comfortable. The middle is when I get most of what I need. The last ten, once everyone has forgotten me, is usually where the best shots live.

I think of myself as a portrait artist first. Classic backdrops, careful composition, quiet retouching. Wall art from family sessions is the most popular print order at the studio, by a lot.

Generational sessions

Grandparents too.

Generational portraits are one of the most-booked sessions at the studio. Three generations in one frame, sometimes four. I book these well ahead, especially when out-of-town grandparents are flying in for the holidays or a birthday.

Sibling-only sessions work too. Just tell me who's in the frame and what you want captured. I'll style around it.

What's included

Every session

  • 01. 60–90 minute studio session with Chana.
  • 02. Group portraits and individual portraits of each family member.
  • 03. Styling guidance sent when you book.
  • 04. Professionally retouched digital gallery in 2 weeks.
  • 05. Heirloom print and wall art ordering.

Common questions

Frequently asked

How long is a family session?
Sixty to ninety minutes. Longer for generational sessions with multiple households.
Do you photograph in-home?
Family sessions are studio-only. In-home is available for newborns under specific conditions.
What should we wear?
A style guide is sent when you book. Coordinated palettes in soft neutrals photograph best in studio.
Can we include grandparents?
Yes. Generational sessions are one of the most common bookings.
When should we book?
Two to four weeks ahead. Holiday season books faster, so aim earlier in the fall.
