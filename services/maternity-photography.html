Maternity Sessions

Baltimore maternity photographer.

I do maternity in the studio only, usually between 32 and 36 weeks. Gowns are here. Drapes are here. You don't need to shop. Partners and older kids are welcome to jump in for a few shots.

Inquire about a session

When to book

Between 32 and 36 weeks.

Third trimester is the sweet spot. You're clearly showing. You're still moving okay. You haven't hit the point where nothing's comfortable. Thirty-two to thirty-six is the window most of my clients land in.

A lot of families book maternity and newborn together, which I recommend. Pick both dates at once so the full arc is planned before baby arrives. It also makes the newborn session feel like less of a scramble.

How I style

Classic, not trendy.

I lean classic on these. Flowing gowns, warm light, simple backdrops. The goal is a portrait you'll still love in ten years, not one that shouts 2026. Nothing that dates itself.

Sessions run about an hour. Your partner's welcome. Older siblings too. The studio has gowns sized through your third trimester, so you don't need to shop for anything.

What's included

Every session

  • 01. 60-minute private studio session.
  • 02. Gowns, drapes, and jewelry from the studio wardrobe.
  • 03. Portraits of you solo, with partner, and with older children if desired.
  • 04. Professionally retouched digital gallery in 2 weeks.
  • 05. Print and wall art ordering from your private gallery.

Common questions

Frequently asked

When should I schedule maternity photos?
Between 32 and 36 weeks. You're clearly showing but still comfortable on your feet.
Can I bring my own gown?
Yes. The studio wardrobe is provided if you prefer not to shop. Many clients use a mix.
Do you do outdoor maternity?
Sessions are studio-only. I don't offer outdoor maternity.
Can my partner and older children be in the photos?
Absolutely. Many families do a mix of solo and group portraits.
When will I see the gallery?
Two weeks after your session.
