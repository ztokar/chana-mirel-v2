Local to Towson

Towson families at the studio.

Most Towson families deliver at GBMC or Mercy. Both are a short drive from my studio. We coordinate around your discharge date and the five-to-fourteen-day newborn window. Sometimes it's tight. I've done sessions on day eight, on day thirteen, and once on day four.

A lot of Towson clients book the whole arc: maternity in the third trimester, newborn at two weeks, cake smash at one year. It's a natural rhythm and it saves planning time if you're trying to capture all three stages.
