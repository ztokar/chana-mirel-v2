Local to Canton

Canton families at the studio.

Canton families tend to book early. I'll get a call during someone's second trimester, usually right after the twenty-week ultrasound. We hold the spot. Once baby arrives, we pick the actual date inside the five-to-fourteen-day window.

Families from O'Donnell Square, Boston Street, and the waterfront have all found me over the years. Some came through Google. Most came through a friend who'd already booked a session, which is the best compliment I get.

Directions

about fifteen minutes from Canton to the studio.

The studio is at 2376 Sweet Meadow Road, Baltimore MD 21209. It's about fifteen minutes from Canton.

Take I-95 north from Canton or side streets through Highlandtown, then right onto Park Heights Avenue. Sweet Meadow Road is a quiet residential street off Park Heights. Park out front.

Nearby landmarks: Canton Waterfront Park, Patterson Park, O'Donnell Square.

What you can book

All session types run out of the same studio.

Canton clients book everything I offer. Newborn for the first two weeks. Sitter around six months. Cake smash at one. Maternity in the third trimester. Family sessions whenever. Headshots when someone needs an updated bio photo.

Same studio every time. Same photographer. I do my own retouching, so the look stays consistent whether you're booking your first session or your fifth.
