FAQ

Questions, answered.

The most common questions from new clients. Still missing something? Send me a note.

Booking

How far in advance should I book a newborn session?
Newborn sessions book three to six weeks ahead. Reach out during your second trimester to reserve a window around your due date. I hold the spot on my calendar and confirm once your baby arrives.
When is the best time for newborn photos?
Between five and fourteen days old. Baby still curls naturally, sleeps deeply, and poses well. After two weeks, sessions are still beautiful but the look is different.
How far ahead should I book a cake smash or family session?
Two to four weeks is plenty for cake smash, family, and maternity. Holiday slots book faster.

Session day

How long does a newborn session take?
Plan for two to three hours. That covers feeding breaks, outfit changes, and sibling or parent shots. I don't rush. Your baby sets the pace.
Do I need to bring outfits or props?
The studio provides wraps, bonnets, swaddles, backdrops, and cake-smash outfits. Family and maternity clients receive a styling guide when they book.
Do you offer in-home sessions?
Sessions happen in my Pikesville studio. In-home is available for an additional fee in limited cases. The studio is heated, private, and stocked with everything a newborn session needs.

Gallery delivery

When will I see my gallery?
Your private online gallery is ready in two to three weeks. Cake smash and family sessions often deliver closer to two weeks.
Can I order prints and wall art?
Yes. The gallery includes museum-quality print options and album design. I also ship digital files. Most clients choose a mix.

Policies & location

Where is the studio?
2376 Sweet Meadow Road, Baltimore MD 21209. Parking is out front. Families drive in from Canton, Towson, Owings Mills, and across Baltimore County.
What's your cancellation policy?
Life with babies is unpredictable. Cancellations and reschedules are handled individually with no penalty for genuine emergencies. I'll send the details when you book.
